Date: 2026-02-03
Title: Worker Node NotReady
Severity: CRITICAL
Component: nodes

Problem:
worker02.ocp.eidikointernal.com went into NotReady state during
peak hours causing workload disruption.

Root Cause:
kubelet service crashed due to disk pressure on the node.
/var/lib/containers was 95% full causing kubelet to stop.

Resolution Steps:
1. Check node status and conditions:
   oc get nodes
   oc describe node worker02.ocp.eidikointernal.com

2. SSH into the node and check disk usage:
   df -h /var/lib/containers

3. Clean up unused container images:
   crictl rmi --prune

4. Restart kubelet:
   systemctl restart kubelet

5. Verify node returns to Ready:
   oc get nodes -w

Outcome: Resolved. Node returned to Ready after disk cleanup.
Time to resolve: 30 minutes
