Date: 2026-03-10
Title: CrashLoopBackOff in openshift-ingress
Severity: CRITICAL
Component: pods

Problem:
router-default pod in openshift-ingress namespace stuck in
CrashLoopBackOff causing all ingress traffic to fail.

Root Cause:
Invalid certificate was mounted into the router pod.
Certificate had expired causing TLS handshake failures.

Resolution Steps:
1. Check pod status and logs:
   oc get pods -n openshift-ingress
   oc logs router-default-xxx -n openshift-ingress

2. Check the ingress certificate:
   oc get secret router-certs-default -n openshift-ingress -o yaml

3. Delete and regenerate the certificate:
   oc delete secret router-certs-default -n openshift-ingress

4. Force router pod restart:
   oc rollout restart deployment/router-default -n openshift-ingress

5. Verify pods are running:
   oc get pods -n openshift-ingress -w

Outcome: Resolved. Router pod restarted with fresh certificate.
Time to resolve: 20 minutes
